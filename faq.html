Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How much do your services cost?

Answer

The cost of our services depends on what product you are buying. Most products are priced at an hourly rate, rates can be viewed on our website on the product page.

Q: Do you offer discounts?

Answer

We have seasonal offers, offers range from 5-20% of final purchases and typically occur during holiday seasons, black friday etc.

Q: Will I get a personal accountant if I buy your product?

Answer

All of our products will pair you with at least one of our qualified CFPs. You may also request for a specific accountant (based on past experiences or industry experience), and we will do our best to pair you with the accountant of your choice.

Q: What is your return policy?

Answer

If you are at all unsatisfied with our service, we are willing to give you a 100% refund on your payment (within the first 10 days following your first consultation). If you request a return after this period, you will provided with credit (i.e. a 100% refund that must be used on Accuracy products).

Q: When can I consult with my CFP?

Answer

Our CFPs are open during business hours (Mon-Fri 9am-5pm excluding federal holidays), however you may arrange personal meetings with your CFP outside of these hours.
